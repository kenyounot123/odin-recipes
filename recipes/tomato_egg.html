Tomato Egg

Description

My mother used to make this classic Chinese egg dish for our family of 5 all the time! She added some beef to her version, but my Chinese Tomato Egg Stir-Fry is meatless. It was delicious paired with s ome steamed rice and still brings back warm memories around the dinner table today

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Cook the tomatoes
  2. Cook the eggs
  3. Season the eggs
  4. Put everything together
  5. Put ketchup and salt/sugar if necessary
